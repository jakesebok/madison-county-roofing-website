The shop

Same family.
Same county.

Madison County Roofing & Home Improvements has been putting roofs on this part of Illinois since 1937. A family business, doing the same trade, in the same county.

Today the company is run by Mark Eck. Jan and Kelly run the office. Mark shows up to every single job: to scope it, to check on it, to follow up himself. Every crew member has been with the company ten years or more.

The cleanup gets compliments in customer reviews almost as often as the work itself. That's not by accident.
