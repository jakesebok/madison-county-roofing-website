The promise

The same family.
The same county.

Madison County Roofing has been putting roofs on this part of Illinois since 1937. Three generations of customers. The same name on the truck. The kind of business that doesn't need to chase storms because every neighbor in Collinsville already has the number.

Today the company is run by Mark Eck. His sons work alongside him. Jan and Kelly run the office. The crew has been together for over a decade. Mark shows up to every single job — to scope it, to check on it, to answer your questions himself.

The cleanup gets compliments in customer reviews almost as often as the work itself. That's not by accident.
