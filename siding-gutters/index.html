What exterior work does
Madison County Roofing handle?

Last updated July 12, 2026

Madison County Roofing installs and replaces siding, seamless gutters, soffit, and fascia across the Metro East, not just roofs. A single storm often damages all four at once, so the same crew addresses the entire exterior envelope on one timeline instead of leaving homeowners to juggle three separate contractors.

A great roof only matters if the rest of the exterior is doing its job. Bad gutters dump water against the foundation. Cracked fascia lets squirrels into the attic. Tired siding warps the wood underneath until you've got a wall problem, not a siding problem.

We've been doing siding, gutters, soffit, and fascia alongside the roofing work for as long as the company has existed. Often these are the same job — hail damage takes out the roof and the siding and the gutters. We do them all at once, on one timeline, with one crew.

According to the Insurance Information Institute, wind and hail are among the most common causes of home insurance claims, and damaged siding and gutters often accompany a damaged roof. Madison County Roofing repairs all three in one trip.

Exterior services

What we install & replace.

Vinyl siding

Siding repair & full replacements

From patching a few damaged panels after a hailstorm to wrapping the whole house in fresh vinyl. We pull the old material, repair any rotten sheathing underneath, install house wrap, and put the new siding up — straight, level, and properly j-channeled.

Seamless gutters

Custom-cut on site

Aluminum gutter sections cut to length on a truck-mounted machine right at your house. No splices to leak. Sized for your roof's drainage volume. K-style or half-round profile, every standard color.

Soffit & fascia

Closing in the eaves

The boards under your roof overhang (fascia) and the panels under that (soffit) keep weather out and ventilation flowing. We replace damaged wood, install vented vinyl soffit, and wrap fascia in aluminum trim coil for a clean, weatherproof finish.

Gutter guards

Less ladder time

Mesh or solid covers that keep leaves out so you're not cleaning gutters every fall. Several systems — we'll tell you which one actually works for your trees and your slope.

Common questions

Siding & gutters,
the common questions.

Do you do siding repairs or only full replacement?

Both. We replace individual hail-damaged or weather-worn panels, and we do full re-sides on older homes. If you have a fresh roof going on, it's often the right time to address siding too.

What gutter sizes do you install?

Standard 5-inch and oversized 6-inch seamless aluminum gutters, cut to your roofline on-site with a truck-mounted machine. We also install gutter guards and downspout extensions.

Can you replace soffit and fascia at the same time as the roof?

Yes. Soffit and fascia work pairs naturally with a reroof since the crew is already up on the eaves. Often it's cheaper to do them together than separately.

What kind of siding do you install?

Primarily vinyl siding. We can match what's already on your house or upgrade the whole exterior envelope.

Do you install gutter guards?

Yes. Gutter guards are a common add-on with new seamless gutters. We typically recommend them for homes with overhanging trees.

Do you do interior remodeling?

No. We're Madison County Roofing & Home Improvements — but the 'home improvements' piece covers the exterior: soffit, fascia, trim carpentry, light exterior remodels. Not interior work like kitchens or bathrooms.
