1937
Era one · The founding

Roofing Madison County
before it was Madison County.

Madison County Roofing was founded in 1937. That's older than the interstate that runs through Collinsville. Older than zip codes. Older than asphalt shingles being the standard roofing material in America.

What's stayed constant for eighty-seven years is the same thing that's true today: this is a family business, doing the same trade, in the same county. Madison County homeowners across multiple generations have called this number. A surprising number of customers got our name from their parents.

2026
Era two · Today

Mark Eck, the team,
and one parking lot in Collinsville.

Today the company is owned and run by Mark Eck. Family members work alongside Mark in the field. Janet — most customers call her Jan — runs the office and schedules every estimate. Kelly handles the books. Stephanie keeps the rest of the operation moving.

Mark is on every single job site. Not as a sales gimmick — because that's the only way he knows how to run it. He scopes the work himself. He meets the adjusters himself. He follows up with you himself.

The crew has been together for a decade or more. Every technician on the roof has 10+ years of experience. That continuity is the difference between a company that's been in business since 1937 and a company that calls itself "established" because it bought the same domain in 2018.
